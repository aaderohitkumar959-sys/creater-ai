# Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Last Updated**: December 16, 2025

---

## 1. General Policy

**Subscriptions and coin purchases are generally non-refundable**, except as specified below or required by applicable law.

## 2. Subscription Refunds

### 2.1 NEW Subscribers (First Purchase Only)
**48-Hour Satisfaction Guarantee**:
- If this is your first subscription purchase
- Request refund within 48 hours of initial purchase
- Full refund, no questions asked
- One-time opportunity per user

### 2.2 Service Outages
Full or partial refund if:
- Service unavailable for 72+ consecutive hours
- Major features unavailable for 7+ consecutive days
- Refund prorated based on downtime

### 2.3 Billing Errors
Full refund if:
- Charged incorrectly (wrong amount, duplicate charge)
- Charged after cancellation
- Request within 60 days of charge

### 2.4 NOT Refundable
- Partially used subscription periods
- Buyer's remorse after 48-hour window
- Violation of Terms resulting in account termination
- Dissatisfaction with AI response quality

## 3. Coin Purchase Refunds

### 3.1 Generally Non-Refundable
Coin purchases are **final and non-refundable** because:
- Virtual currency has immediate value
- Prevents abuse and fraud
- Technical limitations in reversing coin usage

### 3.2 Exceptions
Refunds MAY be granted for:
- **Unauthorized Purchase**: Evidence of fraud or unauthorized access
- **Technical Error**: Coins not delivered after successful payment
- **Accidental Purchase**: Within 10 minutes, no coins spent
- **Billing Error**: Charged wrong amount

**How to request**: Email support@creatorai.com within 24 hours with:
- Transaction ID
- Payment receipt
- Reason for refund
- Screenshots (if applicable)

## 4. Subscription Cancellation

### 4.1 How to Cancel
**Via Website**:
1. Log in → Settings → Subscription
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Receive email confirmation

**Via Email**:
- Email: support@creatorai.com
- Subject: "Subscription Cancellation - [Your Email]"
- Include: Account email, subscription tier

### 4.2 When Cancellation Takes Effect
- **Immediate Access Loss**: No (you keep access until period end)
- **Effective Date**: End of current billing period
- **Auto-Renewal**: Stops immediately
- **Coins/Benefits**: Remain until period end

### 4.3 Reactivation
- Can reactivate anytime before period ends
- Previous tier and benefits restored
- Billing resumes at next cycle

## 5. Chargebacks & Disputes

### 5.1 Our Policy
- **Contact us first**: Most issues resolved quickly
- **Chargeback consequences**: Account suspended pending resolution
- **Fraudulent chargebacks**: Permanent account termination

### 5.2 Dispute Process
If you dispute a charge with your bank:
1. Your account is **immediately suspended**
2. We provide evidence to payment processor
3. If chargeback succeeds: Account terminated, no future access
4. If chargeback fails: Account restored after fees paid

**Better approach**: Contact support@creatorai.com first

## 6. Refund Processing

### 6.1 Timeline
- **Approval**: Within 3-5 business days
- **Processing**: 5-10 business days
- **Bank posting**: Varies by bank (usually 3-5 days)
- **Total time**: Up to 20 business days

### 6.2 Refund Method
- **Same payment method** used for purchase
- Stripe processes refund
- No cash refunds

### 6.3 Partial Refunds
For service outages:
- **Calculation**: (Downtime hours / Total period hours) × Subscription price
- **Minimum**: No refund if downtime < 24 hours
- **Maximum**: Full subscription price

## 7. Special Circumstances

### 7.1 Account Termination
If we terminate your account for violations:
- **No refund** for current subscription period
- **No refund** for unused coins
- **No appeal** for refund

### 7.2 Service Discontinuation
If we shut down the Service:
- All active subscribers receive **prorated refunds**
- Calculated from shutdown date to period end
- Automatic processing, no request needed

### 7.3 Legal Requirements
We comply with:
- **EU Consumer Rights**: 14-day cooling-off period (digital content exceptions apply)
- **State Laws**: California, New York, etc.
- **Credit Card Network Rules**: Visa, Mastercard policies
