# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated**: December 16, 2025

---

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy defines prohibited conduct on CreatorAI. Violation may result in account suspension or termination.

## 2. Prohibited Content

### 2.1 Child Safety (Zero Tolerance)
- **STRICTLY PROHIBITED**: Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- **PROHIBITED**: Sexualization of minors
- **PROHIBITED**: Grooming or predatory behavior toward minors
- **PROHIBITED**: Age-play scenarios involving minors

**Violation consequences**: Immediate permanent ban + law enforcement reporting

### 2.2 Illegal Activities
- Terrorism, violent extremism, or radicalization
- Human trafficking or exploitation
- Sale of illegal goods (drugs, weapons, etc.)
- Financial fraud or scams
- Copyright infringement or piracy
- Hacking, phishing, or cybercrime instruction

### 2.3 Harmful Content
- Graphic violence, gore, or torture
- Self-harm, suicide encouragement, or pro-anorexia content
- Dangerous challenges or harmful instructions
- Coordinated harassment or brigading

### 2.4 Hate & Harassment
- Hate speech based on race, religion, gender, sexuality, disability, etc.
- Targeted harassment, bullying, or doxing
- Threats of violence or intimidation
- Sexual harassment or unwanted sexual content

### 2.5 Deceptive Practices
- Impersonation of individuals or organizations
- Misleading or deceptive information
- Spam, phishing, or malware distribution
- Fake accounts or coordinated inauthentic behavior

## 3. System Abuse

### 3.1 Technical Exploitation
- Attempting to bypass content moderation
- Scraping, crawling, or unauthorized data extraction
- Reverse engineering or decompiling the Service
- DDoS attacks or intentional service disruption
- Exploiting bugs for unfair advantage

### 3.2 Payment Abuse
- Fraudulent payments or chargebacks
- Coin duplication or exploitation
- Account sharing for monetary gain
- Reselling accounts or coins

## 4. Content Moderation

### 4.1 Automated Systems
- AI-powered content filtering (OpenAI Moderation API)
- Keyword and pattern detection
- Behavioral analysis and anomaly detection

### 4.2 Reporting
- Users can report violations via report button
- Reports are reviewed within 24-48 hours
- False reports may result in penalties

### 4.3 Enforcement Actions
- **Warning**: First minor violation
- **Temporary Suspension**: 3-30 days for serious violations
- **Permanent Ban**: Severe or repeated violations
- **Law Enforcement**: Reported for illegal content (CSAM, terrorism, etc.)

## 5. User Responsibilities

### 5.1 Personal Safety
- Do not share personal information (address, phone, SSN, etc.)
- Do not arrange in-person meetings with AI characters
- Report suspicious or concerning behavior

### 5.2 Content You Create
- You are responsible for content you input
- Do not use the Service to generate harmful content
- Respect intellectual property rights

### 5.3 Respectful Interaction
- Treat others with respect
- No harassment of staff or moderators
- Follow community guidelines

## 6. Specific Use Cases

### 6.1 Educational Use
- ✅ ALLOWED: Using AI for learning and education
- ✅ ALLOWED: Academic research (with disclosure)
- ❌ PROHIBITED: Cheating on exams or assignments

### 6.2 Creative Writing
- ✅ ALLOWED: Fiction and creative storytelling
- ✅ ALLOWED: Role-playing (age-appropriate)
- ❌ PROHIBITED: Content violating this policy

### 6.3 Commercial Use
- ✅ ALLOWED: Using AI output for business (check ToS)
- ❌ PROHIBITED: Reselling access to the Service
- ❌ PROHIBITED: Competing service based on our data

## 7. Age Restrictions

- **Minimum Age**: 18 years old (or 13+ with parental consent)
- **Age Verification**: May be required for certain features
- **Mature Content**: Age-restricted content requires 18+

## 8. Consequences of Violations

### 8.1 Account Actions
- Content removal
- Feature restrictions
- Account suspension (temporary or permanent)
- Coin/subscription forfeiture (no refund)

### 8.2 Legal Actions
- Cooperation with law enforcement
- Civil liability for damages
- Criminal prosecution for illegal activities

### 8.3 Appeal Process
- You may appeal enforcement decisions via email
- Appeals reviewed within 7 business days
- Final decisions are at our discretion

## 9. Updates to This Policy

- We may update this policy at any time
- Changes effective immediately upon posting
- Continued use constitutes acceptance

## 10. Reporting Violations

### 10.1 In-App Reporting
- Use the "Report" button on any message
- Select violation type and provide details
- Automated review + human review for serious cases

### 10.2 Email Reporting
- Email: abuse@creatorai.com
- Include: Username, violation type, evidence (screenshots)
- Response within 48 hours

### 10.3 Emergency Situations
- **Immediate danger**: Contact local law enforcement (911)
- **CSAM**: Report to NCMEC (cybertipline.org)
- **Then**: Report to us for account action

## 11. We Reserve the Right To

- Remove content without notice
- Suspend or terminate accounts without refund
- Report illegal activity to authorities
- Preserve content as evidence
- Update policies at any time

**By using CreatorAI, you agree to this Acceptable Use Policy.**

**Violations can be reported at: abuse@creatorai.com**
