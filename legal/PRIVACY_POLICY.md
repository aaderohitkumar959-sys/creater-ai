## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability**: Export your data in machine-readable format
- **Restriction**: Limit processing of your data
- **Objection**: Object to processing for certain purposes
- **Withdraw Consent**: Stop marketing communications

### 7.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)
- **Know**: What data we collect and how it's used
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your data
- **Opt-Out**: Opt out of data "sales" (we don't sell data)
- **Non-Discrimination**: Not be discriminated against for exercising rights

### 7.3 How to Exercise Rights
- **Account Deletion**: Settings → Account → Delete Account
- **Data Export**: Settings → Privacy → Export Data
- **Email Us**: privacy@creatorai.com
- **Response Time**: Within 30 days
