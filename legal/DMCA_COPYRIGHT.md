## 5. DMCA Takedown Procedure

### 5.1 For Copyright Holders
If you believe your work is being infringed, send a DMCA notice to:

**DMCA Agent**:
Email: dmca@creatorai.com  
Address: [YOUR BUSINESS ADDRESS]

**DMCA Notice Must Include**:
1. Your physical or electronic signature
2. Identification of copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
3. Identification of infringing material (URL, username, specific content)
4. Your contact information (address, phone, email)
5. Statement of good faith belief that use is not authorized
6. Statement that information is accurate under penalty of perjury
7. Statement that you're authorized to act on copyright owner's behalf

**Example Notice**:
```
To: dmca@creatorai.com
From: [Your Name]

I am the copyright owner (or authorized to act on behalf of the owner) of the following work:

[Description of copyrighted work]

I have found unauthorized use of this work on your platform at:
[URL or description of location]

I have a good faith belief that this use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

I declare under penalty of perjury that this information is accurate and that I am authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

Signature: [Your signature]
Date: [Date]
Contact: [Address, phone, email]
```

### 5.2 Our Response
Upon receiving valid notice, we will:
1. **Remove or disable** access to allegedly infringing content
2. **Notify** the user who posted the content
3. **Terminate** accounts of repeat infringers
4. **Process** within 24-48 hours

## 6. Counter-Notice Procedure

### 6.1 If Your Content Was Removed
If you believe your content was removed by mistake or misidentification:

**Send Counter-Notice To**:
Email: dmca@creatorai.com

**Counter-Notice Must Include**:
1. Your physical or electronic signature
2. Identification of removed content and location before removal
3. Statement under penalty of perjury that content was removed by mistake
4. Your contact information
5. Statement consenting to jurisdiction of federal court
6. Statement you'll accept service of process from complainant

**Example Counter-Notice**:
```
To: dmca@creatorai.com
From: [Your Name]

The following content was removed from my account:
[Description and former location]

I declare under penalty of perjury that the content was removed by mistake or misidentification.

I consent to the jurisdiction of the Federal District Court for the district in which my address is located, and I will accept service of process from the person who filed the original DMCA notice.

Signature: [Your signature]
Date: [Date]
Contact: [Address, phone, email]
```

### 6.2 Reinstatement Process
After receiving valid counter-notice:
1. We forward it to original complainant
2. If no court action within **10-14 business days**, content may be restored
3. Final decision at our discretion
