## üìã Documents Included

### 1. [Terms of Service](./TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md)
**Purpose**: Legal agreement between you and users  
**Covers**:
- User eligibility and account creation
- Prohibited conduct and content policies
- Virtual currency (coins) and subscriptions
- AI-generated content disclaimers
- Intellectual property rights
- Liability limitations and dispute resolution
- GDPR data rights and privacy

**Key Sections**:
- Age restrictions (18+, or 13+ with parental consent)
- Zero-tolerance policy for CSAM
- No warranties on AI accuracy
- Arbitration clause (consider your jurisdiction)
- Refund policy summary

---

### 2. [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md)
**Purpose**: Disclose data collection and usage practices  
**Compliance**: GDPR, CCPA, COPPA  
**Covers**:
- What data we collect (account, chat, usage, payment)
- How we use data (service, improvement, safety)
- Third-party sharing (AI providers, payment processors)
- User rights (access, deletion, export, portability)
- Data security measures
- International data transfers
- Cookie policy

**Key Sections**:
- GDPR rights for EU users
- CCPA rights for California users
- COPPA compliance (no users under 13)
- Data retention periods
- Security measures (encryption, access controls)

---

### 3. [Acceptable Use Policy](./ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md)
**Purpose**: Define prohibited content and behavior  
**Covers**:
- Prohibited content (CSAM, illegal activity, hate speech)
- System abuse (scraping, exploitation, fraud)
- Content moderation process
- Enforcement actions (warnings, bans)
- Reporting mechanisms

**Key Sections**:
- Zero-tolerance for CSAM (immediate ban + law enforcement)
- Hate speech and harassment policies
- Technical exploitation prohibitions
- Three-tier enforcement (warning ‚Üí suspension ‚Üí ban)

---

### 4. [Refund & Cancellation Policy](./REFUND_POLICY.md)
**Purpose**: Set expectations for refunds and cancellations  
**Covers**:
- Subscription refund conditions
- Coin purchase refund exceptions
- Cancellation process
- Chargeback policy
- Processing timelines

**Key Sections**:
- 48-hour satisfaction guarantee (new subscribers only)
- Generally non-refundable (subscriptions & coins)
- Service outage refunds (72+ hours downtime)
- Billing error refunds

---

### 5. [DMCA & Copyright Policy](./DMCA_COPYRIGHT.md)
**Purpose**: Handle copyright infringement claims  
**Compliance**: DMCA (Digital Millennium Copyright Act)  
**Covers**:
- Copyright ownership (platform, user content, AI content)
- DMCA takedown procedure
- Counter-notice process
- Repeat infringer policy
- Trademark usage

**Key Sections**:
- AI-generated content is not copyrighted
- DMCA agent contact information
- Three-strike repeat infringer policy
- Safe harbor protection
